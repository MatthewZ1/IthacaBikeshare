Cornell Users: Trips by Hour

The usage of Ithaca Bikeshare for Cornell users shows a clear pattern. The activity is low at night, starts to rise in the morning around 8 AM and peaks in the afternoon, around 2 PM. After 5 PM, it starts to decline. This shows that most Cornell students use Ithaca Bikeshare for midday travel, likely to commute to classes.

Cornell vs. Other Email Affiliation

Over 60% of all Ithaca Bikeshare users use Cornell affiliated emails. Considering Cornell users who do not use Cornell emails for Ithaca Bikeshare, we can tell that Cornell users are take up a significant portion of all Ithacka Bikeshare users.
